About Kerala

Kerala is one of the small states of India in terms of area. it consists of only 1.3 per cent of the total area of India. The state is located in the southwest corner of India. Karnataka and Tamil Nadu bound it on north, east and south and on east by the Arabian Sea. Kerala- Named as one of the ten paradises of the world by National Geographic Traveler is the most popular tourist destinations in the country. Its unique traditions and culture, blended with its varied demography, have transformed Kerala one of the most visited and explored tourist destination in the world.
